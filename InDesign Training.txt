Picas vs points vs inches
  1 pica = 1/72 of a foot or to 1/6 of an inch
  12 points = 1 pica
  6 picas = 1 inch
  1 point = 1 pixel on a 72ppi display, 72 pixels = 1 inch
  1 point = 1/2 pixel in a 144ppi document, 144 pixels = 1 inch
